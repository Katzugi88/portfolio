Acoustic Band formed since 2017

A professional band playing all around Doha, Qatar.

Band Profile

We are a band of 4 members Formed in 2017, members Nell (Lead Guitar), Edson (Rhythm Guitar), Riber (Bass) and Glenn (Vocals and Cajon). Light but crisp music covers on hand, this group consistently raise popularity in Doha local music scene. Exposures include sparking up concerts of famous personalities and events, Qatar Events under Qatar Tourism & Qatar Supreme Committee (QIFF & Arab Cup), Front act performances in International Bands and performing in a well known bars and clubs here in Doha entertaining high class crowd (The Mix Bar, Rose and Thistle, Happy Place, The Shamrock Tavern, Krossroads Doha & Wakrah, Misturado and many more). We have a wide genre of songs mainly includes pop, rock, reggae, country to name a few. Playlists come from legendaries like Beatles, Eagles, Oasis and many more.
